## Goal

Cockroach activity is reduced by removing food and water, using gel bait correctly, sealing entry points, monitoring results, and escalating when infestation signs are heavy.

## Preconditions

- Supplies: gloves, trash bags, sealed food containers, caulk or steel wool, cockroach gel bait, bait stations or sticky monitors.
- Pets and children can be kept away from bait placements.
- You can inspect kitchen, bathroom, trash, and appliance gaps.

## Steps

1. **Confirm the activity.** Look for live roaches, droppings like black pepper, egg cases, shed skins, and odor. → *Expect:* the problem area and severity are clearer.
2. **Remove food access.** Seal pantry goods, wipe counters, clean under appliances, and take out trash nightly. → *Expect:* roaches have less loose food than bait.
3. **Remove water access.** Fix drips, dry sinks at night, and empty pet water if practical. → *Expect:* fewer water sources remain overnight.
4. **Skip repellent spray near bait.** Do not fog or spray baseboards where gel will go. → *Expect:* roaches are not driven away from bait or contaminated by repellent.
5. **Place gel bait in small dots.** Put pea-sized dots in cracks, hinge corners, under sinks, behind appliances, and near droppings. → *Expect:* bait is hidden along travel paths, not smeared in open areas.
6. **Seal entry and harborages.** Caulk cracks, add door sweeps, and pack pipe gaps with steel wool before sealing. → *Expect:* travel routes narrow and hiding spots shrink.
7. **Set monitors.** Place sticky monitors behind appliances and under sinks, then date them. → *Expect:* catches show where activity continues.
8. **Review after 1-2 weeks.** [BRANCH: fewer catches | same or more] refresh bait if fewer; call a pro if activity persists or spreads. → *Expect:* results are measured instead of guessed.

## Decision points

- Seeing roaches in daytime → likely heavy infestation, call a licensed pest professional.
- Apartment building → notify management, because untreated neighboring units can reinfest yours.
- Pets or toddlers present → use enclosed bait stations and inaccessible gel placements.
- Food business or healthcare setting → use professional pest control immediately.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 More roaches seen after baiting:** bait may be drawing activity out → keep sanitation strict and monitor for a week.
- **F2 Bait dries untouched:** placement is wrong or spray contaminated it → clean area and place fresh dots near droppings.
- **F3 Roaches return after improvement:** entry points remain → reseal pipe gaps and coordinate with neighbors or building staff.
- **F4 Child or pet contacts bait:** remove access and follow product label poison-control instructions → use stations going forward.

## Verification

After two weeks, monitors show sharply reduced or no roach catches, food and water sources are controlled nightly, and any persistent infestation has a professional scheduled.

## Variations

- German cockroaches: focus on kitchens, bathrooms, warmth, and repeated bait refreshes.
- Outdoor roaches entering: door sweeps and exterior gaps matter more.
- Shared housing: assign trash, dishes, and counter-cleaning responsibilities clearly.

## Safety & privacy

Medium risk from pesticides and allergens. Use labeled products only, keep bait inaccessible to children and pets, and avoid broadcasting infestation details beyond people who need to act.
