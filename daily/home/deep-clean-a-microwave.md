## Goal

The microwave interior, door, and turntable are clean, deodorized, and free of loosened food debris.

## Preconditions

- A microwave-safe bowl, water, white vinegar, sponge or cloth, and dish soap.
- The microwave is empty except for the turntable.
- Paper towels or a dry cloth are available for finishing.

## Steps

1. **Make a steam bowl.** Mix 1 cup water with 2 tablespoons white vinegar in a microwave-safe bowl. → *Expect:* the bowl is no more than half full.
2. **Heat until steamy.** Microwave 3 to 5 minutes, stopping when the window fogs heavily. → *Expect:* steam coats the walls and door.
3. **Let it sit closed.** Leave the door shut for 5 minutes so grime softens. → *Expect:* dried splatters look wet or swollen.
4. **Remove hot items carefully.** Take out the bowl and turntable with a dry towel. → *Expect:* the cavity is open and safe to wipe.
5. **Wash the turntable.** Clean it in the sink with dish soap, then dry it. → *Expect:* the glass plate has no grease film.
6. **Wipe top to bottom.** Wipe ceiling, sides, floor, and door gasket with a damp cloth. → *Expect:* softened food lifts without hard scraping.
7. **Treat stuck spots.** Press the warm damp cloth on stuck food for 30 seconds, then wipe again. → *Expect:* residue releases without scratching.
8. **Dry and deodorize.** Dry surfaces, reinstall the turntable, and leave the door open 5 minutes. → *Expect:* vinegar smell fades and no damp puddles remain.

## Decision points

- Vinegar odor bothers you → use lemon slices in water instead.
- Burnt smell remains → repeat steaming, then leave a bowl of baking soda inside overnight.
- Paint or coating is peeling → stop cleaning and consider replacing the appliance.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Bowl boils over:** detect water on the floor of the microwave, recover by unplugging if safe and wiping after it cools.
- **F2 Greasy smear:** detect a cloudy film, recover with a drop of dish soap on the cloth, then rinse-wipe.
- **F3 Turntable wobble:** detect grinding or rocking after reinstalling, recover by seating the ring and plate in the center.
- **F4 Persistent odor:** detect smell after drying, recover with an overnight open box of baking soda.

## Verification

The microwave has a dry clean interior, a seated turntable, and no visible food splatter on the ceiling, walls, or door.

## Variations

- No vinegar: use plain water plus lemon peel.
- Heavy grease: add one drop of dish soap to the wiping cloth, not to the steam bowl.
- Shared microwave: wipe the exterior buttons and handle last.

## Safety & privacy

Low risk from steam and hot glass. Do not use metal scrubbers, do not spray cleaner into vents, and avoid soaking the control panel.
