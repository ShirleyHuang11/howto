## Goal

Get out of bed at the first alarm without negotiating with the snooze button.

## Preconditions

- A realistic wake time that allows enough sleep if bedtime is kept.
- An alarm device loud enough to hear from across the room.
- A first morning destination, such as the bathroom, window, or kettle.

## Steps

1. **Choose one wake time.** Use the same time most days, including weekends within about an hour. → *Expect:* the alarm time is set once, not debated nightly.
2. **Put the phone or alarm across the room.** Place it far enough that turning it off requires standing. → *Expect:* you must leave the bed to stop the sound.
3. **Prepare the first move before bed.** Put slippers, glasses, robe, or water where your feet land. → *Expect:* the first action has no search step.
4. **Let light reach you fast.** [BRANCH: window available | dark morning] Open curtains before bed if private, or use a lamp on a timer. → *Expect:* light appears within the first minute of waking.
5. **Use the one-move-out-of-bed rule.** When the alarm rings, sit up, put both feet on the floor, and stand before thinking about how you feel. → *Expect:* your body is vertical before the argument starts.
6. **Turn off the alarm while standing.** Do not carry it back to bed. ⚠️ *Irreversible:* avoid placing the alarm somewhere hazardous, such as near stairs, hot appliances, or a wet bathroom floor. → *Expect:* alarm is off and you are still upright.
7. **Go directly to the first destination.** Bathroom, window, sink, or water first; messages come later. → *Expect:* one non-bed action is complete within two minutes.
8. **Keep the wake time after a bad night.** Make bedtime earlier later, but do not pay the debt by snoozing. → *Expect:* the anchor survives one rough morning.

## Decision points

- You slept fewer than 5 hours → get up for obligations, but reduce driving risk and plan an earlier night.
- You repeatedly sleep through alarms → add a second alarm with a different sound and check bedtime, not just volume.
- Someone else shares the room → use a vibrating watch or quieter alarm near you, plus a standing task.
- You are waking exhausted despite enough hours → screen for sleep quality problems.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Alarm returns to bed:** detect the phone in your hand under covers, recover by charging it farther away tonight.
- **F2 Snooze math:** detect bargaining for 9 more minutes, recover by standing first and thinking later.
- **F3 Dark-room collapse:** detect crawling back under covers, recover with timed light or curtains opened before sleep.
- **F4 Weekend reset:** detect Monday feeling like a new time zone, recover by limiting weekend drift.

## Verification

The alarm was stopped once, you stood up before checking messages, and you completed the first morning destination without using snooze.

## Variations

- Phone alarm: disable snooze if your app allows it, or use a barcode or walking alarm only if it does not create stress.
- Winter mornings: combine a lamp timer with warm clothing beside the bed.
- Household wake-up: coordinate alarm volume so the plan is firm without punishing others.

## Safety & privacy

Low risk. Do not create obstacle courses while half-awake. If morning sleepiness is severe enough to affect driving or work safety, treat it as a sleep problem, not a character problem.
