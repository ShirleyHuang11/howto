## Goal

Bangs are trimmed conservatively while dry, with a soft edge and enough length left for correction.

## Preconditions

- Hair is dry and styled the way it is normally worn.
- Sharp hair shears, comb, clips, mirror, and good lighting are available.
- You can stop with imperfect but wearable bangs rather than chasing symmetry.

## Steps

1. **Section only the bangs.** Clip back all hair that should not be cut. → *Expect:* a clear triangle or fringe section falls forward.
2. **Comb into natural fall.** Let cowlicks and part lines sit where they normally sit. → *Expect:* the hair hangs without being pulled tight.
3. **Set a longer guide.** Choose a target below the final length, often at the bridge of the nose first. → *Expect:* there is visible extra length for correction.
4. **Twist lightly.** Gather the middle section, twist once, and hold with low tension. → *Expect:* the center hairs are grouped without stretching.
5. **Snip vertically.** Point shears upward and take tiny vertical cuts into the ends. → *Expect:* length shortens gradually with a soft edge.
6. **Release and check.** Comb down, shake hair loose, and compare both sides. → *Expect:* the bangs are longer than final but closer to shape.
7. **Point-cut the edges.** Trim tiny vertical notches where bulky or uneven. → *Expect:* the line softens without jumping shorter.
8. **Stop early.** ⚠️ *Irreversible:* cut hair cannot be put back; stop when bangs are slightly long. → *Expect:* the fringe is wearable and still adjustable.

## Decision points

- Hair is curly or wavy → cut less because dry curl springs shorter.
- You want blunt bangs → still point-cut tiny amounts, then refine the line.
- Bangs are already too short → stop cutting and style with clips or product.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Too short:** detect brows fully exposed unexpectedly, recover by stopping and styling to the side while it grows.
- **F2 Uneven corners:** detect one side higher, recover by softening the longer side only a few hairs at a time.
- **F3 Chunk missing:** detect a notch, recover by point-cutting around it instead of making the whole fringe shorter.
- **F4 Wet-hair shrink:** detect bangs rising after drying, recover next time by cutting dry only.

## Verification

After combing and shaking hair into place, bangs sit at a wearable length with no single hard notch drawing attention.

## Variations

- Curtain bangs: cut the center shortest and angle sides gradually downward.
- Curly bangs: trim individual curls in their dry clumps.
- Thick bangs: remove bulk with small vertical cuts, not a straight horizontal chop.

## Safety & privacy

Medium risk because the result is visible and not immediately reversible. Use hair shears, keep points away from eyes, and stop before frustration changes your judgment.
