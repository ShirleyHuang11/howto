## Goal

Food is cooked in an air fryer in a single hot-air layer, shaken or turned, and the basket is cleaned after use.

## Preconditions

- Air fryer on a heat-safe counter with space around vents.
- Food cut to similar size, light oil if needed, and tongs.
- Manufacturer instructions for maximum fill and removable parts.

## Steps

1. **Place the fryer safely.** Set on a stable heatproof surface with vents clear. → *Expect:* hot exhaust will not blow into a wall, curtain, or hand path.
2. **Preheat if useful.** Preheat 3-5 minutes for frozen fries, chicken, or crisp foods. → *Expect:* basket is hot before food enters.
3. **Oil lightly.** Toss food with 1-2 tsp oil per 500 g unless already fatty or battered. → *Expect:* surface is barely glossy, not dripping.
4. **Load one layer.** Put food in a loose single layer or no more than the marked fill line. → *Expect:* air can move around pieces.
5. **Set time and temperature.** [BRANCH: fries 190 °C 12-18 min | chicken pieces 190 °C 16-25 min | vegetables 180 °C 8-15 min] → *Expect:* timer starts and fan noise is steady.
6. **Shake or turn midway.** Pull basket, shake fries or turn larger pieces, then return promptly. → *Expect:* browning becomes more even.
7. **Check doneness.** Look for browning and use a thermometer for meat. → *Expect:* poultry reaches 74 °C and reheated leftovers reach 74 °C.
8. **Remove carefully.** Dump or lift food onto a plate; do not touch the basket metal. → *Expect:* food is crisp and basket remains stable.
9. **Clean after cooling.** Once warm, wash basket and tray with soapy water and wipe the cavity if greasy. → *Expect:* no crumbs or oil film remain to smoke next time.

## Decision points

- Food is crowded → cook in batches; crowding steams instead of crisps.
- Breaded food looks dry → mist lightly with oil before cooking and again halfway.
- Smoke appears → stop, check for pooled fat or crumbs, and clean once cooled.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Pale food:** detect by soft surface at end time → add 2-5 minutes and use less food per batch.
- **F2 Burnt edges raw center:** detect by dark outside and hard inside → lower temperature and cut pieces smaller.
- **F3 Basket sticks:** detect by torn coating → oil the food lightly and wait a minute before turning.
- **F4 Persistent smoke:** detect by smoke after cleaning basket → wipe the upper heating area after unplugging and cooling.

## Verification

Food is browned and cooked through, the air fryer is off, and the basket and tray are cleaned after cooling.

## Variations

- `reheating`: use 160-180 °C for pizza, fries, and fried foods until hot and re-crisped.
- `frozen-snacks`: start from frozen and reduce package oven time by roughly 20%.
- `parchment-liner`: use perforated liners only with food weighing them down.

## Safety & privacy

Medium risk from hot baskets, steam, and grease smoke. Do not spray aerosol oil into a hot basket, block vents, or put loose paper inside during preheat.
