## Goal

Melt sugar into smooth amber caramel while avoiding crystallization, burning, and molten-sugar burns.

## Preconditions

- Granulated sugar in a clean heavy pan is ready and smells fresh.
- Water or lemon for wet method, plus long sleeves and dry mitts are ready.
- A clean work surface is available.
- Storage or serving container is ready.

## Steps

1. **Choose method.** [BRANCH: dry method | wet method] dry uses sugar only; wet uses water to dissolve sugar → *Expect:* method is chosen
2. **Load pan.** Spread sugar evenly, or stir wet sugar only before heating → *Expect:* pan sides are clean
3. **Heat still.** Use medium heat and avoid stirring once boiling → *Expect:* syrup bubbles clear
4. **Control crystals.** Brush wet-method crystals from the sides with water → *Expect:* syrup stays smooth
5. **Watch color.** Cook from pale gold to amber, knowing color equals flavor → *Expect:* smell turns toasted
6. **Stop early.** Remove at target amber. ⚠️ *Irreversible:* hard-crack dark caramel burns fast and cannot be fixed → *Expect:* color deepens slightly off heat
7. **Finish safely.** Add cream off heat from arm length or pour onto a heatproof mat → *Expect:* violent bubbling settles glossy

## Decision points

- Light sauce -> stop at honey amber.
- Brittle or flan -> go deeper amber but stop before smoke.
- Crystals -> add water and redissolve while still pale.
- Children nearby -> wait until the area is clear.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Crystallized:** detect cloudy grains -> add water and restart without stirring.
- **F2 Burned:** detect smoke or black streaks -> cool, discard, and do not taste molten sugar.
- **F3 Seized:** detect hard lumps after cream -> warm gently until dissolved.
- **F4 Skin burn:** detect contact -> cool under running water and seek care; do not peel hot sugar off.

## Verification

caramel is smooth amber with no crystals or black streaks

## Variations

- Wet method: slower and forgiving.
- Dry method: faster with stronger flavor.
- Salted caramel: add warm cream, then butter and salt.
- Shards: pour thinly on silicone and cool fully.

## Safety & privacy

High risk from molten sugar, which sticks to skin and causes severe burns.
Use dry mitts, keep the area clear, and never touch or taste caramel until cooled.
