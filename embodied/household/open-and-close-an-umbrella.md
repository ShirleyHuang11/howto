## Goal

The umbrella opens to a locked canopy and closes back to a wrapped bundle without striking people or damaging ribs.

## Preconditions

- Umbrella is away from faces, lamps, shelves, and nearby people.
- Strap or sleeve is loosened before opening.
- Runner button or automatic button is visible.

## Steps

1. **Clear the space.** Hold the umbrella tip down and scan a full arm length around the canopy path. ⚠️ *Irreversible:* a rib tip can hit an eye, so pause if anyone is close. → *Expect:* no person or fragile item is inside the opening radius.
2. **Hold the handle.** Grip the handle with the shaft pointed away from bodies. → *Expect:* umbrella is stable and aimed safely.
3. **Release the strap.** Unfasten hook, snap, or sleeve around the folded canopy. → *Expect:* fabric loosens around the ribs.
4. **Open the canopy.** Slide the runner upward or press the auto-open button. → *Expect:* ribs spread evenly.
5. **Lock the runner.** Push until the runner clicks or rests above the catch. → *Expect:* canopy stays open without hand pressure on the runner.
6. **Close by release.** Press the runner catch or close button while controlling the shaft. → *Expect:* runner begins moving down without snapping into fingers.
7. **Fold the canopy.** Pull ribs and fabric downward along the shaft. [BRANCH: wet | dry] keep wet fabric pointed away from electronics. → *Expect:* ribs nest together.
8. **Wrap the strap.** Smooth panels around the shaft and fasten the strap. → *Expect:* umbrella remains compact when released.

## Decision points

- Wind is strong → point canopy into the wind and avoid opening near others.
- Runner sticks → inspect ribs for crossing before forcing.
- Auto umbrella rebounds → hold both handle and runner area until movement stops.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Runner not locked:** canopy collapses after opening → push runner higher until the catch engages.
- **F2 Rib inverted:** one rib points opposite the canopy curve → close partially and reshape gently.
- **F3 Finger pinch:** runner closes on skin → release pressure, move fingers to handle and fabric edges.
- **F4 Wet spray:** canopy sheds water indoors → close slowly over a mat or sink.

## Verification

The umbrella opens locked, closes under control, and finishes strapped with ribs aligned and no contact with people.

## Variations

- Compact telescoping umbrella: collapse shaft sections after the canopy is closed.
- Stick umbrella: use the manual runner and longer clearance radius.

## Safety & privacy

Medium risk because rib tips and spring movement can injure people nearby. Pause opening or closing when anyone is within the canopy radius.
