## Goal

You list a used item honestly and priced to actually sell, communicate safely with buyers, and complete the handover (in person or shipped) so you get paid without getting scammed.

## Preconditions

- The item, cleaned, plus any accessories, receipts, or original box you can include.
- An account on a marketplace (local or shipping-based) and a way to receive payment.
- Good light and a plain background for photos.

## Steps

1. **Research the real selling price before listing.** Search completed/sold listings for the same item and condition, not just active asking prices. → *Expect:* a realistic price range from what items actually sold for.
2. **Clean the item and gather everything that adds value.** Cables, manuals, box, proof of purchase. → *Expect:* the item is presentable and its extras are staged.
3. **Photograph it well and honestly.** Multiple angles in natural light on a plain background, plus close-ups of every flaw (scratches, wear, missing bits). → *Expect:* a set of sharp photos that show both the good and the defects.
4. **Write a specific, honest listing.** Include brand, model, exact condition, dimensions/specs, what is included, and every known flaw. Honesty here prevents disputes later. → *Expect:* a description a stranger could rely on without surprises.
5. **Set the price with room for offers.** Price slightly above your floor if the platform expects haggling; note your true walk-away number privately. → *Expect:* a listed price plus a private minimum you will accept.
6. **Publish and respond promptly to genuine questions.** → *Expect:* the listing is live and searchable; inquiries arrive.
7. **Screen buyers and refuse off-platform payment tricks.** Ignore "I'll send my shipper", overpayment, or requests to move to gift cards or wire. Keep payment inside the platform's protected system. → *Expect:* you are dealing only with normal buyers paying through the platform.
8. **Complete the handover safely.** [BRANCH: local sale, meet in a public, camera-covered spot (or a police-station safe-exchange zone) in daylight, ideally bring a friend, take cash or confirm instant payment cleared before handing over | shipping sale, ship only after payment clears, to the platform-provided address, with tracking and a photo of the packed item] ⚠️ *Irreversible:* once the item leaves your hands you cannot recover it, so confirm real payment first. → *Expect:* item handed over only after confirmed payment; tracking uploaded if shipped.
9. **Confirm funds landed and close out.** → *Expect:* the money is in your account (not just "pending" in a spoofed screenshot) and the listing is marked sold.

## Decision points

- Buyer offers well above asking and asks for a refund of the "extra" → classic overpayment scam; decline entirely.
- High-value item → prefer a platform with seller protection and tracked shipping over a cash local meet.
- Buyer pressures for your home address or a private location → keep it public; a real buyer accepts a safe meeting spot.

## Failure modes & recovery

- **F1 Fake payment confirmation:** detect a "receipt" email or screenshot but nothing in your actual account → never release the item on a screenshot; verify the balance in your own app first.
- **F2 Chargeback after shipping:** detect a reversed payment weeks later → this is why tracking and packing photos matter; submit them to the platform's seller protection.
- **F3 No-show or lowball flood at meetup:** detect a buyer who does not appear or renegotiates on the spot → set a firm price, do not travel far, walk away without regret.
- **F4 Buyer claims item "not as described":** detect a return/dispute → your honest photos and flaw list are your defense; respond with the original listing evidence.

## Verification

The item has been handed over or shipped with tracking, confirmed payment has actually settled in your account, and the listing is closed with no open dispute.

## Variations

- `us`: platforms report seller income above a threshold to tax authorities; keep records of what you sold and for how much.
- Local cash sale: many jurisdictions offer police-station "safe exchange zones" with cameras, the safest meetup option.

## Safety & privacy

Medium risk from both scams and personal-safety exposure. Never share your home address, meet strangers in public daylight spots, verify funds independently before releasing goods, and wipe personal data off any device you sell (factory reset, remove accounts and SIM).
